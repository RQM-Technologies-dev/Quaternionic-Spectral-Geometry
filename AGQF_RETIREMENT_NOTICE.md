# AGQF Retirement Notice

Status: `retired`

The Anchor-Generating Quaternionic Factorial (AGQF) was an exploratory dressing
of the quaternionic factorial intended to model anchor shells and resonance
wells. It is not Vladimir Fock's hydrogen hypersphere, is not required to derive
the hydrogen spectrum, and has not produced an independently calibrated physical
prediction.

AGQF is therefore removed from active QSG course objectives, navigation, product
claims, and physical interpretation. Historical derivations, visualizations, and
the pre-Canon-v2 course snapshot remain available for research provenance.

Historical material is not active RQM Technical Canon v2. Relevant evidence is
recorded in EXP-007 and the EXP-008 negative controls in `rqm-experiments`.
