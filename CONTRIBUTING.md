# Contributing

Contributions are welcome when they make the framework clearer, smaller, more formal, or easier to test.

## Claim Discipline

Please distinguish:

- definitions,
- conjectures,
- examples,
- computations,
- references,
- interpretation.

Do not make grand claims without derivations, proofs, examples, or references. Keep the repository focused on mathematical objects, maps, operators, and examples.

## Preferred Contributions

Preferred contributions include:

- clearer definitions of $M = S^3 \times \mathbb R$,
- explicit maps into $M_2(\mathbb C)$,
- hypersphere coordinate examples,
- small spectral-operator models,
- bounded-commutator calculations,
- spectral well and coherence definitions,
- references to established literature,
- formalization feasibility notes.

## Discussion

Use issues for discussion. Keep issues focused on one definition, example, theorem target, or reference at a time.

## Tone

Keep the tone rigorous, open, humble, and research-oriented. The purpose of the repository is to make QSG easier to inspect and improve.
